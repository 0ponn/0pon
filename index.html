about this project

about

The internet remembers everything. Every message archived, every listen tracked, every interaction attributed and stored forever.

These tools push back.

Conversations that fade like spoken words. Music experienced without the bias of knowing who made it. Notes that live in a URL, not on someone else's server.

Not hiding. Just removing the weight of permanence, identity, and surveillance that the default internet assumes you want.
